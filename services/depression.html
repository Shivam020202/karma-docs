Understanding the Condition

More Than Just
Sadness

Clinical depression is a serious mental health condition that affects how you feel, think, and handle daily activities. It is persistent, lingering for weeks or months, and often cannot be simply "willed away."

At Karma Doctors & Associates, we understand the heavy burden of depression. Our team uses scientifically-proven treatments to correct the chemical and neural imbalances causing your symptoms, offering hope even when treatments have failed in the past.

Types of Depression

Depression has many faces. Accurate diagnosis is the cornerstone of our successful treatment plans.

Major Depressive Disorder

Severe symptoms that interfere with your ability to work, sleep, study, eat, and enjoy life.

  • Persistent Sadness
  • Loss of Interest
Get Help

Persistent Depression

A depressed mood that lasts for at least two years. You may have moments of relief but feel consistently "low" or "down".

  • Chronic Fatigue
  • Low Self-Esteem
Get Help

Seasonal Depression

Depression that comes and goes with the seasons, typically starting in the late fall and early winter and going away during the spring.

  • Winter Onset
  • Social Withdrawal
Get Help
