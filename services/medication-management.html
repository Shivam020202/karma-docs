Common Questions

How often do I need to see the doctor?

Initially, follow-up appointments may be more frequent (e.g., every 2-4 weeks) to monitor your response to new medications. Once you are stable, appointments are typically scheduled every 3 months.

Can I stop taking medication if I feel better?

It is crucial to consult your doctor before stopping any medication. Stopping abruptly can cause withdrawal symptoms or a relapse of your condition. We can guide you on how to safely taper off if appropriate.

Do you treat children and adolescents?

Yes, we provide medication management services for adolescents and adults. Please contact our office to discuss specific age ranges and requirements.
